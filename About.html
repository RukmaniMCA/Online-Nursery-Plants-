About Us

Our Dhanya Nursery is located on the main NH47 in Vijayamangalam (near toll gate) in Perundurai (Erode dt).

Mr. K. Jayaprakash, proprietor, has vast experience in the field for the past 20 years and we remember "Our Roots," that is,

Roja Nursery, Papini, Kangayam. It is one of the well-known Nurseries from the 90's. We are producing quality seedlings like Coconut Tall,

Coconut Chowghat Orange, Pkm Tamarinds, Sapotas, Amla, etc., to meet the needs of the farmers.

Our Dhanya Nursery Garden is easy to reach (since it is on the main NH47) and we meet the demands of resellers like bulk purchasers,

nursery and landscape people. We do both retail and wholesale activities. We have a wide range of mass collections of plants, tools, and accessories.
